Championship Info

The ‘05 FOOTBALL FIESTA 2025 is set to make history as an exclusive football tournament for alumni of Sekolah Berasrama Penuh (SBP) and Maktab Rendah Sains Mara (MRSM) born in 2005. Organized by Xanthrons under SASEROBA (SASER Alumni Association), this inaugural event is more than just a competition—it’s a symbol of unity, sportsmanship, and the legacy of Malaysia’s top institutions.

Supported by the Selangor State Government through Y.B. Mohd Najwan bin Halimi, Exco for Youth, Sports, and Entrepreneurship, the tournament is expected to attract hundreds of participants and thousands of spectators, including alumni and the local community. With participation exclusive to 2005-born alumni, this event also provides a unique opportunity for sponsors to engage with ambitious and talented youth—the future leaders of Malaysia.

SASEROBA, established on October 11, 2016, has united nearly 1,000 SASER alumni, making it a prestigious platform for networking, sports, and leadership development. Through this tournament, we aim to promote a healthy lifestyle, foster sportsmanship, and instill positive values that extend beyond the football field. Our goal is clear: to establish ‘05 FOOTBALL FIESTA 2025 as an iconic football event that creates a lasting impact on the community while elevating sponsors' brands among sports fans and the youth of Malaysia. 🚀⚽
